Endline Data This dashboard presents real endline survey data from the Pakistan Home-Based Worker follow-up study. All respondent information is anonymised.

Study at a Glance

An endline follow-up survey of home-based garment workers (wives) and their husbands across households in Pakistan. The study examines changes in women's employment, earnings, household decision-making, time use, and psychological wellbeing — with separate matched instruments for wives and husbands.
